About me

Hey! My name is Ania (Username: Ania da Peasant) and I’m currently a 19-year-old college student living in Melbourne Australia.

For the last 4 years, I’ve been involved in creating content for Youtube. My videos are transformative as I, with permission, take comics and incorperate my voice acting and video editing skills to turn an image into a moving video. Now as a student I want to build myself up with skills and knowledge so I’ve decided to create a portfolio to show what skills I currently have and who I am as a person.

Any and all the work that I currently do I want to do for free as for me right now the most important thing is learning, building skills and the ability to put it into my portfolio and say ‘I’ve made this and my client is satisfied with it’.

Originally I planned to describe in words the last few years online but I struggled to come up with the right words so I created a video about it which is linked here.

So instead I shall write down my motivations and goals, not only so you can understand me more as a person but so I can look back at the words to remind myself what I’m working towards. To an outside person, they may seem selfish or be weird in nature but in the end, it is still my own goals.

Motivations

My wish to understand who I am as a person and where I belong

The need to constantly improve not only myself as a person but my skills and knowledge

Knowing that I CHOSE to be a creator by myself and wasn’t forced/pushed to it

To learn what I enjoy doing and my strengths when doing them

To achieve my short and long term goals

Goals

I want to be proud of my username

I want to be proud of the content that I’ll produce

I don’t want to burdened by past influences telling me 'You can’t create'

I want to put my 100K Youtube subscriber plaque on the wall and see it as an achievement

I want to one day stand on the stage of VidCon and say ‘I did it, I’m here which means you guys can be here as well’

I want to express myself through my work

I want to be ‘Ania da Peasant’ and show my progress from the ground up
